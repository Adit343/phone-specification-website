About Us

Apple Inc. is a technology company based in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, software, and online services. The company is most well-known for its iPhone, a line of smartphones that revolutionized the mobile phone industry when it was first introduced in 2007. The iPhone quickly became one of Apple's flagship products, and the company has continued to release updated models with new features and improvements each year. Apple was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, and originally focused on producing personal computers. Over time, the company has expanded into a variety of other product lines, including tablets, smartwatches, and home automation devices. Today, Apple is one of the largest and most profitable companies in the world, with a market capitalization of over $2 trillion.
